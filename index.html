Hi, I'm Maanav Dalal!

It would be pretty easy to put words like "Talented", "Exceptional", "Driven", and "Leader" over here, and call it a day. But that wouldn't be much fun, would it? Aside from these impersonal adjectives, I'd like to think of myself as a good friend, an avid gamer, and a programmer who's always looking for something interesting to try out.

From HTML or CSS to Java and Node.JS, to even PHP and MongoDB I really have delved in many different programming areas, whether they be front-end or back-end. Just throwing frameworks and programming languages isn't a good scalar of my capability however, so if you're interested I'd invite you to look at my Creations for a more in-depth look. This said, I'd still consider myself just an intermediate programmer who understands concepts and has a pretty good time learning languages and ideas, but I don't think I've reached mastery in any particular fields, with Java being the language I'm most comfortable with.

Programming aside, I love to play games and hang out with my friends, whether that be a late night game of League of Legends, or a morning get-together for a trip to a mall. I'm dedicated to performance in school and getting involved in my community, mainly through extracurricular activities such as Leadership conferences (namely START, SOAR, and IBMC), youth councils - namely BMYC, or clubs such as DECA.
